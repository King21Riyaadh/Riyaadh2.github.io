Marketplace simulations
is a program that simulates a competitve market against other real individuals. During my time in this project I was in a team that was marketing for a high-end bicycle company. My team competed against four other teams that started off with identical companies. We had control over many variables like the quality and price of the bikes as well as how many workers we would hire, development and advertising expenses, which market to market to, number of retail locations and many more. The goal of the simulation to make a profitable company and to try and gain the most amount of market share as possible. At the end of the project, my team and one other were dominating in market share holding roughly 70% of the market together. It was very close competition but when it was over my team came second place. Everyone received a digial badge certificate as proof of being involved in the simulation. Overall it was a fun project that let me apply bussiness concepts and techniques I had learned.
